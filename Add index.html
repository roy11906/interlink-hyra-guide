🪙 InterLink 註冊與挖礦教學

InterLink Registration & Mining Guide

  1. 手機商店下載 InterLink Network，然後打開
    Search and download “InterLink Network” from your app store, then open it
  2. 點選「同意」使用條款
    Tap “Agree” to accept the Terms of Service
  3. 點選右上角「頭像」 → 點「登錄」 → 點「註冊」
    Tap the profile icon (top-right) → tap “Login” → tap “Register”
  4. 設定用戶 ID（4–12 個字元）、密碼，並再次確認
    Set your user ID (4–12 characters), password, and confirm it
  5. 綁定一個通訊軟體（如 fb、Telegram）
    Bind your account with a messaging app (e.g., fb or Telegram)
  6. 點選「推薦朋友」 → 「Human Hash」 → 輸入推薦碼：76660，輸入後按右邊的【提交】按鈕
    Go to “Invite Friends” → “Human Hash” → enter referral code: 76660, then tap the “Submit” button on the right
  7. 完成活體驗證（正臉 → 右臉 → 左臉 → 抬頭 → 低頭）
    Complete the liveness check (front face → right → left → up → down)
  8. 綁定電子信箱
    Bind your email address
  9. 點下方「挖礦」按鈕，每 4 小時點一次啟動
    Tap the “Mine” button at the bottom; activate mining every 4 hours

🤖 Hyra AI 註冊與教學說明

Hyra AI Registration & Daily Guide

你可以到手機商店搜索 Hyra AI 下載
Search “Hyra AI” in your app store to download

  1. 點【登記】
    Tap “Register” to create your account
  2. 輸入電子信箱，推薦碼：46010411218bb，同意條款打勾後選【繼續】
    Enter your email, input referral code 46010411218bb, agree to terms, then tap “Continue”
  3. 註冊成功後點左上角「頭像」，裡面的地球圖示可選自己國家的語言
    After registering, tap the avatar (top-left), then the globe icon to select your language
  4. 完成 KYC 驗證：
    Complete KYC identity verification:
    • 點「頭像」或首頁的任務 → KYC
      Go to avatar or homepage “Tasks” → KYC
    • 輸入本名、出生年月，選擇自己國家
      Enter your real name, birth date, and country
    • 選擇驗證類型（身份證、護照或駕照）
      Choose document type: ID card, passport, or driver’s license
    • 拍攝正反面，清楚、四角入鏡
      Take clear front/back photos with all corners visible
    • 自拍驗證（臉對圓圈）：正面 → 轉左臉 → 轉右臉
      Face selfie: front → left → right
  5. 完成電子信箱認證
    Verify your email
  6. 每 12 小時可點一次訓練模型；底下小模型可開自動，也能連接電腦或 Telegram 訓練
    Every 12 hours, tap “Train Model”. You can enable auto-training or connect to PC/Telegram
  7. 任務區每日簽到與追蹤官方帳號（點了可回來，30 分鐘後再去領獎）
    Daily tasks include check-in and following official accounts (tap → return → claim reward after 30 minutes)
