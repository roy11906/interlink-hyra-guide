InterLink Registration & Mining Tutorial

手機商店下載 InterLink Network→然後打開

Download "InterLink Network" from the app store and open it.

點選「同意」使用條款

Tap “Agree” to accept the terms of use.

點右上角「頭像」 → 點「登錄」 → 點「註冊」

Tap the top-right avatar → Tap “Login” → Tap “Register”.

設定用戶 ID（4–12 字元）、密碼，並再次確認

Set a user ID (4–12 characters), password, and confirm the password.

綁定 facebook 或 Telegram

Bind your Facebook or Telegram account.

再次點選「頭像」進入「帳戶管理」 選擇語言

Tap the avatar again → Go to “Account Settings” → Choose your language.

點「推薦朋友」點選「Human Hash」 輸入推薦碼：76660，並按右邊的提交

Tap “Refer a Friend” → Choose “Human Hash” → Enter referral code: 76660 and tap the button on the right.

完成活體驗證（正臉 → 右臉 → 左臉 → 抬頭 → 低頭）

Complete the liveness check (Front → Right → Left → Look Up → Look Down).

綁定電子信箱

Bind your email address.

每 4 小時點一次「挖礦」按鈕

Tap the “Mine” button every 4 hours.
